Artist Name

MEJJA

Major Nameye Khadija popularly known with his stage name Mejja is a Kenyan Genge artist. He was signed to Calif Records where he released multiple collaborations. His single "Jana Kuliendaje" won him an award at Kenya's Chaguo la Teeniz in 2008

MORE ABOUT MEJJA

Mejja started singing in primary school with his brother Wambugu. Together they had formed a group called Ghetto Clan. In 2008 Mejja had his first breakthrough song titled ‘Jana Kuliendaje’. In 2011, Mejja released a song dubbed "Landlord" which put him in the spotlight. In 2014, Mejja formed a group called "The Kansoul" together with Kid Kora, and Madtraxx. The group dropped their first song ‘Dabo Tap’ later that year. In 2020, Mejja was in many collaborations including "Cheza Kama Wewe" by Trio Mio[6] and "Utawezana" by Femi One
