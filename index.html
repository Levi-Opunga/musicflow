About Us

Music Flow is website made to give a platform to promote local music.

Our Objective

We believe that the only way to grow our local talent is by first creating an audience for it within the country before thinking outside.

We believe that we can achieve this by promoting streaming of local content on major platforms such as spotify and deezer which will increase the revenue of local artists cause such platforms pay better.

Once this is done we will achieve growth of the music industry because artists will receive value for their content enabling them to expand he industry.

Our hope is to grow the local industry to the point music can be considered a viable career path.
